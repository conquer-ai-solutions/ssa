How the audit trail works
  • Every AI recommendation is logged with timestamp, data inputs, and confidence score at point of generation
  • Human decisions — approve, edit, override — are recorded against each recommendation. This is the feedback loop that improves the model over time
  • Overrides are especially valuable: when a human takes a different action, CDP notes the outcome and recalibrates recommendation weighting accordingly
  • The full log is available for compliance review at any time. Every action is attributable and auditable.
